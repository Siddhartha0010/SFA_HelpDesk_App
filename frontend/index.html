Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I track my incentives and rewards?

To track your incentives and rewards in the SFA 2.0 app:

  1. Navigate to the "My Rewards" section from the main menu
  2. Here you can view your current incentives, potential earnings, and achievement metrics
  3. Check the "My Coins" tab to see your accumulated reward points
  4. View the "My Rank" tab to see your performance ranking compared to other team members
  5. The "Summary" tab provides an overview of your performance across different metrics

Regular monitoring of your rewards and incentives can help you focus your efforts on activities that maximize your earnings.

Where can i see SFA Schemes in the app?

To to check schemes SFA 2.0 schemes:

  1. On the home screen tap on "START DAY"
  2. Here you can view the list of outlets on the screen
  3. Select the desired outlet and click "Begin Visit"
  4. Here you can view the outlet data
  5. Click on the % symbol on the top right corner on the screen to view Schemes applicable to this outlet.

Discussion about schemes with the outlet retailers is important
